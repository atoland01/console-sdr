How I Selected These Accounts

// ICP framework + timing signal methodology

ICP + Timing Signal Framework

I selected these five accounts by starting with Console's ICP – tech-forward mid-market companies, 200–5,000 employees, Slack-native, with IT teams stretched relative to headcount – and then layering on timing signals that suggest a buying window is open right now. A company that fits the profile is a suspect. A company that fits the profile and has a live trigger is a prospect.

For contacts, I mapped each account against the buyer personas visible in Console's published case studies: the champion who feels the pain daily (Head of IT, IT Ops Manager), the economic buyer who controls budget (CIO, CISO, VP), and a side-door contact who can validate the value from an adjacent angle. For outreach, each account gets a 2-week multi-threaded sequence across LinkedIn, email, and phone.

Methodology
🎯
Signal-First Selection
Each account maps to a distinct buying signal: competitive disruption, growth, leadership change, funding momentum, or geographic complexity.
👥
Multi-Thread by Design
Champion + economic buyer + side door per account. No single-threaded deals. Every named contact gets 3+ touches.
✉️
Proof-Led Messaging
Every sequence references real Console case studies (Scale AI, Webflow, Bloomerang) with specific metrics – not generic positioning.
